Aromatic Chicken Curry
Ingredients:
  • 2 lbs chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tbsp TerraFeed Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp TerraFeed Turmeric
  • 4 green cardamom pods
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
Instructions:
  1. Heat oil in a large pan and sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, and cardamom. Cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add garam masala and turmeric. Stir for 30 seconds.
  4. Add chicken pieces and brown on all sides.
  5. Pour in coconut milk and simmer for 25-30 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and serve with rice.
